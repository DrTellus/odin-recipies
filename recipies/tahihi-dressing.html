Tahini dressing

Tahini dressing is delicious on everything from falafels and salads to pizza.

Ingredients

  • Tahini
  • Lemons
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Korean chili flakes
  • MSG
  • Lukewarm water

Steps

  1. Peel the garlic
  2. Squeeze the juice from the lemons
  3. Mix everything together in a blender adding lukewarm water if it gets too thick
  4. Salt to taste
