Hummus

Homemade hummus with secret hack

Hummus is an easy and healty snack for both lunch and dinner as well as late nigh.

In this recipie I'll give away two secrets that elevates your hummus to new heights!

Indredients

  • Drained chickpeas
  • Garlic
  • Cumin
  • Tahini
  • Lemon juice
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

    • And now for our two secret ingredients..

  • Canned jalapeos in brine
  • MSG

Steps

  1. Add everything exept olive oil into a food processor and run until very smooth
  2. Slowly pour in olive oil while the food processor is still running
  3. Salt to taste
  4. Serve
